Our Commitment to

Diversity

Through the Global Diversity Committee and our Global Initiative, we try and bring awareness to the diversity within our local community. Bonita Springs & Estero are fast-growing communities that are becoming more diverse by the year. Join us for our events & classes discussing diversity and use the online recourses here to help you become more aware of our local neighbors and those around the world.
