Why Get CIPS Certified?

The Certified International Property Specialist (CIPS) designation is the gold standard for real estate professionals who want to serve international clients at the highest level.

Higher Earning Potential

International transactions often involve higher price points and unique opportunities. CIPS designees are positioned to capture this lucrative market segment.

Global Referral Network

Access NAR's worldwide network of CIPS designees for cross-border referrals. Connect with professionals in 70+ countries to grow your business.

Serve Diverse Clients

Southwest Florida attracts buyers from around the world. Gain the cultural competency and transaction skills to serve clients from any country.

Stand Out from Competition

Differentiate yourself with a recognized credential. The CIPS designation signals expertise and commitment to international real estate excellence.
