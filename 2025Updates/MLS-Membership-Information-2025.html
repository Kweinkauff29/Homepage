Join the MLS

Welcome! Start by completing the Join BER application. That page also includes current dues info and a dues calculator.

New to BER / SWFLAMLS

  1. Apply via Join BER (includes dues & calculator).
  2. Your Broker of Record approves your request.
  3. Pay initiation + prorated dues; we’ll send your MLS (Matrix) login.
  4. Activate Supra eKEY (see Supra section) and set up showing tools.
Start: Join BER See Recurring Fees

Already a REALTOR® elsewhere?

  1. Obtain a Letter of Good Standing from your primary board.
  2. Complete BER secondary membership + MLS add-on (via Join BER above).
  3. We’ll coordinate reciprocity with your broker as needed.

Tip: Ask your AE to email the letter to Membership@BERealtors.org.

Transfer or Reactivate

Transfer Offices / Brokers

  1. Your new broker updates the license with DBPR.
  2. Email MLS@BERealtors.org to update your MLS office and roster.
  3. We’ll confirm billing moves with Membership.

Data transfer (2025): We can pull your contacts and auto-emails over from NABOR only (RPCRA split their dashboard). Tell Membership you want your old data pulled and we’ll handle it.

Reinstate (Lapsed)

  1. Clear any outstanding balance in the Member Portal.
  2. Request MLS reactivation — we’ll restore access and Supra as needed.

IDX Access (BridgeAPI only)

BER provides IDX via BridgeAPI only. Our IDX policy is contained within the MLS Rules (PDF).

How to Request Access

  1. Vendors: request access to the Bonita Springs–Estero REALTORS® association within BridgeAPI, or email Tech@BERealtors.org to be invited.
  2. We work directly with vendors, not individual agents.
  3. If your vendor already receives an IDX feed: have them email Tech@BERealtors.org with your site details to approve your domain.

What to Know

  • Follow display, attribution, and refresh rules in the MLS Rules document.
  • Ensure broker authorization is current in your vendor’s Bridge integration.
  • For compliance questions, contact MLS@BERealtors.org.
