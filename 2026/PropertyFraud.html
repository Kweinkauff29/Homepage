Simple & Free

How Does It Work?

The Lee County Clerk's Property Fraud Alert is a free service. Simply follow these three steps to protect your property.

1

Click the Link

Visit the Lee County Clerk's Property Fraud Alert registration page below.

2

Register Your Property

Enter your name, business name, or parcel ID to set up automatic monitoring.

3

Get Notified

Receive an email alert within 24 hours after any change is made to your property deed.
