What is Realtor.com+?

Realtor.com+ is a collaboration platform that connects agents and clients in a unified experience.

  • It brings searches, shared listings, and messages under one roof.
  • Designed to keep everyone in sync from first click to closing table.
Explore features Industry announcement

Key Benefits for BER Members

Protected & branded space

Your clients see you. No competing lead forms or other agent advertisements in their view.

MLS-level search filters

Advanced criteria and draw-on-map functionality directly tied to BER MLS data.

Real-time client insights

See their searches, saved properties, and views instantly to guide them better.

In-app chat

Message back and forth natively with clients and co-buyers easily.

Touring requests

Streamlines the showing workflow straight from their saved listings.

Mobile + desktop synced

Work seamlessly anywhere, on any device, with data synced universally.

Note: No changes to your membership benefits or dues are planned—this is an added benefit.

How it works

1

Invite your client from Realtor.com+

Send them an invite directly through your new dashboard.

2

Client accepts & logs in

They accept using their own Realtor.com account credentials.

3

Experience upgraded

Their experience upgrades into Realtor.com+—collaborate entirely in one place.
