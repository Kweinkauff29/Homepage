Why Bonita Springs-Estero REALTORS®?

Home to 1,300+ REALTORS® since 1966, BER pairs big-board resources with a small-board personal touch. Our mission: support success beyond sales for members, clients & community.

Signature Member Perks

  • FREE 16-ft Moving Truck + Cargo Van — book online, wow your buyers & sellers.
  • Media Room Studio — 4K cameras, podcast mics & green-screen backdrops.
  • Realtor Review Magazine — quarterly print with agent-focused articles.
  • Shareable Market-Stats Videos & Page — fresh Bonita-Estero infographics every month.
  • Complimentary coworking desks and meeting rooms.

Technology & MLS Power

  • Realist (CoreLogic) — tax rolls, owners, liens & heat-mapped comps inside Matrix.
  • Matrix MLS + MLS-Touch sync for live data & instant comparables anywhere.
  • Cloud CMA | Agent 3000 — pro CMAs, single-property sites, flyers & drip campaigns.
  • BrokerBay | ShowingTime — AI scheduling & feedback dashboards.
  • Domus Market Stats | RatePlug — local analytics & live mortgage quotes.
  • Form Simplicity / TransactionDesk e-sign, FOREWARN safety & RentSpree screening — all free.

Education & Leadership

  • Full CE calendar: view upcoming classes ›
  • BER News — articles & how-to guides 24/7.
  • BEST Designation — six-module local-expert path + neighborhood walking tour.
  • Leadership Academy — six-month cohort, Tallahassee day & capstone project.
  • 15+ committees open for volunteers (Gov’t Affairs, Global, YPN, Outreach).

Global Reach & Diversity

  • NAR Platinum Global Achievement (2025) — 5 Years in a row. One of 11 nationwide.
  • Trade-mission stipends (Canada • Jamaica • Portugal in 2025).
  • Inbound referral desk + quarterly “Around the Globe” mastermind.
  • Diversity & Inclusion — BER pledge & resources.
  • Sales Excellence Awards — Gold ($10M), Platinum ($20M) & Diamond ($40M) tiers.

Advocacy & Member Support

  • Legal Hotline: Florida REALTORS® free counsel at 407-438-1409.
  • Tech Helpline: FR IT support at 407-587-1450.
  • RPAC Triple Crown — 5 consecutive years exceeding fundraising & participation goals.
  • 2025 Legislative Win: HB 913 (Greater Condo Transparency & Buyer Protections) passed.
  • GARD 2025: Our delegation lobbied in Tallahassee during Great American REALTOR® Days.
  • Advocacy Legacy: Since 1966, championing private-property rights in SW FL.

Community & Networking

  • Sips & Tips: Monthly happy-hour roundtables sharing hot topics.
  • July 4th Parade Float: Annual community showcase on Estero Blvd.
  • Habitat Builds: Quarterly volunteer days making homes a reality.
  • Installation Banquet: Gala induction & Sales Excellence Awards celebration.
  • Advisory Council: Meets first Tuesday monthly at 10 AM to steer BER’s strategy.
