The Unreal Realtor Run for RPAC

Our Inspiration:

Start From Your Couch (Or Actually Run)

Get ready for the most fake race you’ll ever not run! The Unreal Realtor Run for RPAC is a virtual “race” where you can support RPAC without breaking a sweat. Sign up, “pretend” to run, and earn bragging rights—because in this race, everyone's a winner!

But wait—for the real go-getters, we’ve got a real challenge! Compete in our “Most Miles Sold” Challenge to see who can log the most miles in 25 days. Only the top closer will take home the title!

BER is celebrating “Miles to Advance our Mission in May”. Sign up before May 1st and start clocking your miles run and investments between May 1st and May 23rd. Winners will be announced on May 30th. Awards will go out by age group, top brokerage participation, and top RPAC donor.

How It Works

The Fake Race

Step 1: Invest $40 in RPAC at floridarealtors.org/invest and send your receipt to membership@berealtors.org.

Step 2: Register at tinyurl.com/Unrealrun and receive your Unreal Realtor Run bib.

Step 3: Stage your most dramatic “running” photo (bonus points for props like donuts).

Step 4: Post your photo with #UnrealRealtorRun for a chance to win the Funniest Fake Running Photo contest.

Step 5: Get your medal for successfully “finishing” the event!

The "Most Miles Sold" Challenge

Track your miles with any app (Strava, Nike Run Club, etc.) from May 1–23. Submit your results by May 26 at tinyurl.com/RPACRun. Only active Realtors and RPAC donors are eligible.

New Contest: Funniest Fake Running Photo

Show us your most outrageous “running” photo. Think: running in heels, chasing your dog, or battling a cat for a listing.

Post it with #UnrealRealtorRun and tag us in the BER Members Facebook group or upload it at tinyurl.com/RPACRun.

We’ll crown the funniest photo with a special prize and endless bragging rights!

Why Participate?

  • ✔ Support RPAC – Help protect your industry while having fun (or faking it).
  • ✔ Get Recognition – Whether you “run” or really run, you’ll be celebrated.
  • ✔ Bragging Rights – Earn the title of Mileage King or Queen!
  • ✔ Laughs Guaranteed – Because fake races are funny and so are your photos.
