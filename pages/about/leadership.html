Program Commitments

By applying, you agree to the following requirements to ensure a successful experience.

1
Attendance: Commit to attending all program classes. Early departures or late arrivals count as absences. You may not miss more than 2 classes to graduate.
2
Committee Involvement: If not already on a BER committee, agree to regular committee involvement after completing the program.
3
Florida Realtors Convention: Strongly encouraged to attend. BER covers hotel costs for shared rooms and may provide transportation.
4
Class Project: Participate in a Leadership Development Class Project with a community service component, approved by the Leadership Committee.
5
Interview: Attend a pre-selection interview (Feb 17 or 20).
6
Investment: $200 for Realtors (includes shirt, name tag, FR Convention registration). $500 for Affiliates (includes marketing perks). Due by the introductory module.
7
NAR Certification: Complete NAR's Leadership100 at nar.realtor (free) prior to graduation.
