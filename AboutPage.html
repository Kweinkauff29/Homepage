About Southwest Florida (SWFL)

BER serves ALL of Southwest Florida — Bonita Springs, Estero, Naples, Fort Myers, Cape Coral, Marco Island, Sanibel & Captiva, Lehigh Acres, Babcock Ranch, Punta Gorda and more. (See full GEO coverage)

Bonita Springs Estero Naples Fort Myers Cape Coral Marco Island Sanibel & Captiva Lehigh Acres Babcock Ranch Punta Gorda

Bonita Springs — “Gateway to the Gulf”

Gulf-front beaches and waterways with year-round shelling, boating, and fishing—from Bonita Beach to Barefoot Beach Preserve.

Estero

Resort-style communities and world-class shopping/dining at Coconut Point and Miromar Outlets, plus FGCU and Hertz Arena nearby.

Naples

Luxury coastal living, arts & dining on Fifth Ave South and Third Street, golf communities, and Gulf beaches.

Fort Myers

River District core, healthcare & education hubs, historic neighborhoods, and access to RSW and I-75.

Cape Coral

400+ miles of navigable canals, boating lifestyle, family neighborhoods, and a growing small-business scene.

Marco Island

Resort island living with beachfront condos, boating, and eco-tourism on the Ten Thousand Islands.

Sanibel & Captiva

World-class shelling, wildlife refuges, and conservation-minded coastal communities.

Lehigh Acres

Value-focused housing and rapid growth along the SR-82 corridor with quick access to Fort Myers and Immokalee.

Babcock Ranch & Punta Gorda

Solar-powered master-planned living and a historic harborfront downtown with outdoor recreation.

The REALTOR® Three‑Way Membership

Join once, belong to three organizations: your local board Bonita Springs‑Estero REALTORS® (BER), Florida REALTORS®, and the National Association of REALTORS® (NAR). One application, one set of dues, three levels of benefits.

Local: BER

Education & designations, MLS access (Matrix–SWFLAMLS), Supra support, networking, and local advocacy.

State: Florida REALTORS®

Statewide advocacy, legal resources, research & trends, professional development, and industry events.

National: NAR

Code of Ethics, national advocacy, the REALTOR® brand, and member benefits including tools & discounts.
